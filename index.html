System Analysis & Design

A centralized management platform designed for EverGreen Legacy Corp, a Philippine-based insurance and financial services company offering life, legacy, and preneed plans. The system is built around three roles — Branch Managers, Cashiers, and the General Manager — and replaces manual, paper-based processes with one platform.
